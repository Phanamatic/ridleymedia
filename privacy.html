Privacy Policy

How we handle your data when you use our website or contact us.

What we collect

If you submit the contact form, we receive your name, email, message, and any optional fields. This information is used solely to respond to your enquiry and provide services.

Retention

We keep enquiries as long as needed to provide services, maintain records, and comply with legal obligations. We then delete them.

Third parties

We use Cloudflare Turnstile to protect the contact form from spam. If you submit the form, Turnstile may process technical data to detect abuse. We also use privacy-friendly analytics (Plausible) to understand aggregate usage without tracking individuals or using cookies.

Your rights

You can request access, correction, or deletion of your data by emailing chris@ridleymedia.co.za.

Cookies

This site does not use advertising cookies. If analytics or tracking pixels are added later, this policy will be updated and, where required, consent will be requested.

Contact

For privacy questions or requests, email chris@ridleymedia.co.za.
